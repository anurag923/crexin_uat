  • In an unforeseen incident, if the equipment breaks down during the work schedule an alternative will be provided with in 24 hrs from the time of equipment breakdown.
  • If the allocated machine breaks down before the start of work schedule or while transporting to the work location, an alternative will be provided with in 24 hrs from the start of the actual schedule. Else if the customer wish to cancel his booking , he can cancel thru our mobile or web interface for which the total amount paid by the customer will be refunded to the same account within 3 business working days.
